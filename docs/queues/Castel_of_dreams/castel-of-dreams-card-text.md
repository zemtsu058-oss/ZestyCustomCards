# Castel of Dreams Card Text

Text transcribed from the card images in this folder. Spelling and grammar are kept as shown on the cards.

## Bena, Guardian of the Castle of Dreams

- Attribute: WATER
- Level: 4
- Type: Illusion/Effect
- ATK/DEF: 1600/1800

```text
If this card is Normal or Special Summoned: You can Set 1 "Castle of Dreams" Normal Spell/Trap to your field.
If a Field Spell card you control would be destroyed, you can banish this card from your GY, instead.
You can only use each effect of "Bena, Guardian of the Castle of Dreams" once per turn.
```

## Castel of Dreams - Betrayal

- Type: Trap Card

```text
If 2 or more monsters was Special Summoned from your opponent Deck and/or Extra Deck this turn, while you control a "Castle of Dreams" Field Spell: Take control of 1 monster your opponent control, then if you take control of a Effect Monster, that monster gain the following effect: "Once per turn, when your opponent activate a monster effect: Destroy that monster".
You can only activate 1 "Castel of Dreams - Betrayal" per turn.
```

## Castel of Dreams - Breakout

- Type: Trap Card

```text
If 3 or more monsters was Special Summoned from your opponent Deck and/or Extra Deck this turn, while you control a "Castle of Dreams" Field Spell: Destroy any number of card on field, then you can Special Summon 1 monster from your GY or banishment.
You can only activate 1 "Castel of Dreams - Breakout" per turn.
```

## Castel of Dreams - Fall

- Type: Trap Card

```text
If 3 or more monsters was Special Summoned from your opponent Deck and/or Extra Deck this turn, while you control a "Castle of Dreams" Field Spell: Your opponent send cards from their hand or Extra Deck to the GY equal to the number of cards in their GY, then you can place 1 Field Spell from your Deck, GY, or banishment face-up on your field.
You can only activate 1 "Castel of Dreams - Breakout" per turn.
```

## Castle of Dreams - Dream Show

- Type: Spell Card

```text
Add 1 "Castle of Dreams" card from your Deck to your hand, except "Castle of Dreams - Dream Show", then if your opponent control a monster that was Special Summoned from their Deck or Extra Deck, you can draw 1 card.
You can only activate 1 "Castle of Dreams - Dream Show" per turn.
```

## Castle of Dreams - Fairytale

- Type: Field Spell Card

```text
During your Main Phase: You can add 1 "Castle of Dreams" monster from your Deck to your hand.
Each turn, the first time a card or effect resolve that would negate another card effects, change that effect to 1 of there effect (your opponent choice):
(1) Both player draw 1 card.
(2) All monster on field gain 500 ATK.
(3) Both play gain 1000 LP.
You can only use each effect of "Castle of Dreams - Fairytale" once per turn.
```

## Castle of Dreams - Iris's Necklace

- Type: Spell Card

```text
Special Summon 1 "Castle of Dreams" Illusion monster from your Deck or GY, or if your opponent control a monster that was Special Summoned from their Deck or Extra Deck, you can Special Summon 1 "Castle of Dreams" Spellcaster monster instead.
You can only activate 1 "Castle of Dreams - Iris's Necklace" per turn.
```

## Castle of Dreams - Stage

- Type: Field Spell Card

```text
During the Main Phase, if a monster(s) is Special Summoned from the Deck and/or Extra Deck: You can Special Summon 1 "Castle of Dreams" monster from your hand, GY or banishment.
Each turn, the first time a card or effect resolve that would negate another card effects, change that effect to 1 of there effect (your opponent choice):
(1) Both player draw 1 card, then discard 1 card.
(2) All monster on field gain 500 ATK, but change them to Defense Position.
(3) Both play gain 1000 LP, but take 2500 damage during the End Phase of this turn.
You can only use each effect of "Castle of Dreams - Stage" once per turn.
```

## Cautus, Keeper of the Castle of Dreams

- Attribute: WIND
- Level: 4
- Type: Illusion/Effect
- ATK/DEF: 1600/1800

```text
If you control only Spellcaster and/or Illusion monsters, you can Special Summon this card from your hand.
If this card is Normal or Special Summoned: You can excavate the top 6 cards of your Deck, then if you excavated a "Castle of Dreams" card(s), you can add 1 of them to your hand, also place the rest on the top of your Deck in any order.
If a Field Spell card you control would be destroyed, you can banish this card from your GY, instead.
You can only use each effect of "Cautus, Keeper of the Castle of Dreams" once per turn.
```

## Iris, Master of the Castle of Dreams

- Attribute: LIGHT
- Level: 6
- Type: Spellcaster/Effect
- ATK/DEF: 2000/2500

```text
If your opponent control a monster that was Special Summoned from their Deck or Extra Deck, or you control no monster in your Main Monster Zone, you can Special Summon this card from your hand. You can only Special Summon "Iris, Master of the Castle of Dreams" once per turn this way.
Once per turn, during your Main Phase: You can place 1 "Castle of Dreams" Field Spell from your Deck face-up on your field.
Once per turn, when your opponent activate a card or effect, while you control a "Castle of Dreams" Field Spell (Quick effect): That effect become once of there effect (your opponent choice);
(1) Your opponent draw 2 cards, then you draw 1 card.
(2) All monsters on field gain 500 ATK, then all monsters your opponent controls gain 500 ATK.
(3) You opponent gain 2000 LP, then you gain 1000 LP.
```
